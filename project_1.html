Poker 357

Poker 357 is a table game on the 188 Bet platform. This game has two different design styles, one is a gorgeous and luxurious gaming style, and the other is a fresh and cute style.

Features

Poker 357 is a well-known poker game. In this game we will use 52 cards without joker, and reshuffle the cards in each round. Dealer will deal a total of 7 cards, the first three cards will be the first result, and the first five cards will be the second result. As a result, the third result is drawn when there are 7 cards. If the combination of 7 cards is only one short of one to form a Royal Flush / Straight Flush / Four of a Kind, then you can buy the 8th card.
In this project, we will launch two different visual game designs for English and Chinese players.
